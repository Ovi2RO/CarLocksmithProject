A damaged car ignition switch can occur due to wear and tear or as a result of a forced action. Common signs of a damaged ignition switch include the key not turning in the ignition switch, the key not going into the ignition switch, the key not coming out of the ignition switch, inability to turn the key, inability to start the car, no lights on the dashboard, and no crank. These issues indicate that there may be a problem with the ignition switch that needs to be addressed.
